Computer Science and Creative Writing?

Upon learning about my double major, people often ask me what made me want to study both computer science and English. Though the two fields seemingly have little in common, I believe both have a foundation in this idea of a creative commons. In computer science, open source software and technology is available to users so that they can learn from it, use it, and maybe even improve it. In the discipline of writing, language is a common culture that every author draws from and adds to in crafting their work. I am drawn to both fields because they encompass ideas that are important to my personal life in the work place: hard work, creativity, and community.
